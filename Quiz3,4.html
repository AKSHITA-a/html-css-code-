Nature

Nature refers to the inherent character or constitution of the ecosphere or the universe as a whole. In a general sense, it encompasses the laws, elements, and phenomena of the physical world, including life. Humans are part of nature, but at times, human activity is described as being at odds with or even separate from nature.
